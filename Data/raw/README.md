LLM Training Dataset Collection

This repository contains datasets collected for training and evaluating Large Language Models.

Contents:

1. LLM_Papers
   - 100 research papers related to Large Language Models and AI.

2. ArXiv_Metadata
   - ArXiv metadata snapshot containing paper metadata.

3. SQuAD
   - Stanford Question Answering Dataset v2.0.

4. StackExchange
   - AI and DataScience StackExchange dumps including posts, comments, tags, users and votes.

5. HuggingFace_QA_Datasets
   - Multiple QA and instruction datasets including:
     - Dolly 15k
     - Natural Questions
     - ELI5
     - Qasper
     - Arxiv QA

Purpose:
These datasets are collected for research and experimentation in LLM training, QA systems, and instruction tuning.
